Situation / The Maker

A craft chocolate maker ready to be more than a local secret.

Founded in Exeter, 2021. Bean-to-bar, single-origin, award-winning.
Devon inclusions — sea salt, Exmoor tea, local rum & chai.
Founder-led, DTC, ~£4.45 gross margin per bar — but low awareness beyond the Southwest.
The challenge: premium emotional positioning in a crowded £16.18B market.
