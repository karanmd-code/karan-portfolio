Content Creation, University of Exeter

Visit Exeter.
A fresh perspective on the city.

A 3-minute promotional video created for Visit Exeter as part of the Content Creation and Communication module (BEMM779). The brief was to produce an authentic, audience-specific video that could work as both a standalone YouTube ad and a full-length introduction to the city.

The brief

Create an informal 3-minute video for Visit Exeter targeting a specific audience. The video needed to attract visitors, present Exeter in a positive light, engage viewers within the first 10 seconds, work as a standalone 30-second ad on YouTube, and function as a full 3-minute introductory video. The content had to include audio (music and/or narration) and align with Visit Exeter's brand.

My role

Editing

Full post-production in DaVinci Resolve. Colour grading, pacing, transitions, split-screen compositions, and sound mixing.

Ideation and scripting

Concept development, narrative structure, shot listing, and scripting the video's arc from hook to call-to-action.

Shooting

On-location filming across Exeter. Directed shots, framed compositions, and captured B-roll to support the narrative.

The video

Approach

We targeted international students considering Exeter for postgraduate study. The video opens with a search-question hook, transitions through the city's highlights using a mix of narration, music, and split-screen visuals, and closes with a clear call-to-action. My international perspective shaped the storytelling, bringing an authentic angle that the assessor specifically highlighted as engaging and relevant.
