Visit Exeter. A fresh perspective on the city.

A 3-minute promotional video for Visit Exeter, made for the Content Creation & Communication module (BEMM779) — built to work as both a 30-second YouTube ad and a full-length introduction to the city.

The brief

Create an informal 3-minute video for Visit Exeter targeting a specific audience. It needed to attract visitors, present Exeter positively, hook viewers within the first 10 seconds, work as a standalone 30-second YouTube ad, and function as a full 3-minute introduction — with audio aligned to Visit Exeter's brand.

Editing

Full post-production in DaVinci Resolve — colour grading, pacing, transitions, split-screen compositions, and sound mixing.

Ideation & scripting

Concept development, narrative structure, shot listing, and scripting the arc from hook to call-to-action.

Shooting

On-location filming across Exeter — directed shots, framed compositions, and B-roll to support the narrative.

Approach

We targeted international students considering Exeter for postgraduate study. The film opens on a search-question hook, moves through the city's highlights with narration, music, and split-screen visuals, and closes on a clear call-to-action.

Our international perspective shaped the storytelling — an authentic angle the assessor specifically highlighted as engaging and relevant.

Feedback

"My favourite elements were the sections where your humour and personality came across — very appropriate for authentic content."
