About

I think like a marketer.
Shoot like a filmmaker.

I'm a content marketer and video creator drawn to the space between strategy and craft — where intent meets image.

MSc Marketing from the University of Exeter, with freelance experience producing over 100 assets across fitness brands, lifestyle clients, and community projects — all shot, directed, and edited end-to-end in DaVinci Resolve.

I'm drawn to cinematic expression, even in simple ideas. A gym floor, a workout, a chocolate melting — the interesting part is finding the frame that makes it feel like something.

The craft

Visual storytelling, framing, pacing, mood. Strong attention to detail — the look and the feel matter as much as the message.

The philosophy

Intentional, thoughtful execution over noise or hype. I'd rather make one thing that lands than ten that disappear into the feed.

The instinct

Drawn to dramatic, cinematic expression — finding what's visually interesting in the everyday. Content that stops the scroll because it earned it.

A few things about me

Active: Football most weeks, moving most days — sports keep my head in the right place.

Watching: Films and shows that respect the frame. Cinematography is my favourite form of attention.

Cooking: Slow dinners, trying new things. Beer and coffee take this seriously.

Thinking: Reflective by default — I like sitting with ideas before shaping them.
